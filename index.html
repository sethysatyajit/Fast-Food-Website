Popular Recipes

Veggie Burger

25 min 420 cal

Juicy plant-based patty with fresh veggies and special sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 veggie patty
  • 1 whole wheat bun
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Vegan mayo
  • Spices to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook patty according to package
  2. Toast bun lightly
  3. Assemble with veggies and sauce

Veggie Pizza

35 min 380 cal

Crispy crust loaded with fresh vegetables and vegan cheese

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vegan cheese
  • Bell peppers, mushrooms, onions
  • Italian herbs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F
  2. Roll out dough and add sauce
  3. Add toppings and cheese
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes

Veggie Nuggets

20 min 320 cal

Crispy plant-based nuggets with dipping sauce

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups soy chunks
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 cup flour

Instructions:

  1. Process soy chunks into fine texture
  2. Mix with spices and shape into nuggets
  3. Coat with flour, then breadcrumbs
  4. Bake at 400°F for 15 minutes

Veggie Hot Dog

15 min 290 cal

Classic plant-based hot dog with all the fixings

Ingredients:

  • 2 veggie dogs
  • 2 hot dog buns
  • Mustard, ketchup, relish
  • Chopped onions

Instructions:

  1. Cook veggie dogs according to package
  2. Toast buns lightly
  3. Add dogs to buns and top as desired

Veggie Grilled Cheese

10 min 450 cal

Melty vegan cheese with tomato soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices bread
  • Vegan cheese slices
  • Vegan butter
  • 1 can tomato soup

Instructions:

  1. Butter bread and assemble sandwiches
  2. Cook in pan until golden on both sides
  3. Heat soup according to can instructions

Vegan Ice Cream

10 min (+freeze) 240 cal

Creamy dairy-free ice cream in minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 frozen bananas
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional toppings

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth
  2. Freeze for 2 hours
  3. Add toppings and serve
